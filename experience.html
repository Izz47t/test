My Experiences

Based on my overall experience from sweet to bitter and my lifetime experience that I faced.

Winning the MSSPP title

Back in 2020, my high school team made history by winning the MSSPP title in a Rugby 15s match. After several years of being in second and third place, Alhamdulillah in 2020 we managed to win that Champion title. It was a happy day for me because the day that we won is my birthday and it was very special experience for me. Luckily, that final match was recorded and posted in YouTube by Ragbi Online TV. I would like to share the video with you.

Date: 10th February 2020

Day: Monday

Venue: MRSM Kepala Batas

Shoulder Dislocation

This is one of the craziest injuries I have experienced. It happened when I was 14 years of age. It started when I was attending training and I used the wrong technique to tackle my opponent so he fell on my shoulder. The recovery was long, I couldn't participate in the MSSPP tournament, the saddest thing was I couldn't join my seniors' team in a tournament that is well known in Penang which is USM 10s. I have been rested for 6 months and fully recovered after 7 1/2 months. Alhamdulillah, now I have played in the tournament on 1st and 2nd June 2024.

Working as a sales assistant

I have experience working with one of the biggest sports retailers in Malaysia, Al-Ikhsan. I worked as a sales assistant for about 4 months. I gained experience and increased my knowledge of sportswear. It was a fun experience in that I made new friends and also learned how society works. It means a lot to me that they teach me tons of stuff

Duration: April 2022 - July 2022
